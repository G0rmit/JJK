Full description of Toji Fushiguro

The Inverted Spear of Heaven: special grade cursed tool with the ability to completely nullify cursed techniques on contact.

Immense body strength: because of Heavenly Restriction, his physical power is beyond human. Toji was one of the most elite fighters in the jujutsu world when he was alive. He was able to defeat the strongest jujutsu sorcerers at the time without sustaining any injuries beyond a small cut around the ear, getting a nickname "Sourcerer killer"

Inventory Curse: Toji has a cursed spirit that can store many different objects within its body. Toji can freely interchange his weapons by putting them in and taking them out of the curse's mouth.

Heavenly Restriction: Toji doens't possess any cursed energy, that is a common thing among sourcerers. The reason for this is a Heavenly Restriction, binding vow, placed on his body at birth. While in most cases it apmlifies cursed energy and weakens body, this time it was reversed.

Personality

Toji Fushiguro is cool-headed, confident, and enjoys insightful conversations. He is an assassin who doesn't question his client's motives as long as the money is right. Spends his time gambling between assignments from shady clients. Hates sourcerers as they disregarded him in childhood because of his lack of cursed energy.
